Garlic Toast

Description

Garlic bread is a must have when making many italian dishes such as lasagna. With this classic recipe you are sure to wow your guests.

Ingredients

  • italian herb bread loaf
  • pesto
  • soften salted butter
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cut your loaf of bread into half inch slices
  3. Cover with soften butter and pesto
  4. Sprinkle cheese over it
  5. Place your garlic bread onto a non-stick sheet
  6. Place in over for 10 minutes or until golden brown
