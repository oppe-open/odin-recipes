Salsa

Description

The best homemade salsa you'll ever have. So good you'll want to slap your mama for not making it sooner!

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • garlic
  • onion
  • cilatro
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cut all your ingredients into small enough pieces to fit into your blender
  2. Put all your ingredients into the blender and mix until a consistance you like
  3. Heat the blended mixture to a boil and let simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Once it has a deep red color it is done and can be cooled to serve. (Add salt as needed)
